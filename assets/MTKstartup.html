Welcome to MTKutility

This app will only work with a GPS device has the MTK GPS chipset.

To use this app you need to pair your GPS device in your phone’s Bluetooth settings, and select the paired GPS device in app Preferences.

Once you have your logger paired, you need to tell this app about it by selecting it in this app's Preferences. Tap the Preferences button in the Action Bar (the wrench symbol), then tap on GPS device and select your GPS. Once this is done you will be able to connect to your GPS device via Bluetooth.

Make sure you click on the information button (i with a circle around it) to learn how to use MTKutility.

Please email the developer at adt.androidapps@gmail.com if MTKutility does not work with your GPS device. The app has extensive execution tracing built in that will help resolve issues with your devices.
